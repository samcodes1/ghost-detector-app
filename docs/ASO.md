# Ghost Detector App — Play Store ASO Kit

Drop these straight into Google Play Console. Every field is designed around the
keywords people actually search for on the Play Store in this category, weighted
by Play's known ranking signals (Title > Short Description > Long Description >
icon CTR > install velocity & reviews).

Primary keyword target: **ghost detector**
Secondary cluster: ghost camera, ghost prank, ghost finder, ghost radar,
ghost hunter, spirit box, paranormal scanner, AR ghost, ghost in camera, EMF
detector, haunted, ghost prank app, scare prank, real ghost detector.

## 1. App Title (max 30 chars)

Play Console field: **App name**.
Pick ONE — they're ordered by ranking strength for the primary keyword.

| Option | Chars | Notes |

Play's Title and the launcher label are independent — Play title is what users
see in search results and on the listing page.

## 8. Data Safety declarations (Play Console)

Be honest — Play rejects apps that under-declare:

- **Data collected**: Advertising ID (via AdMob), approximate device info (ad SDK).
- **Data shared**: with Google AdMob for ads.
- **Data security**: data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS via AdMob); user
  can request deletion by uninstalling.
- **Camera data**: processed on device for AR only; **not collected, not
  transmitted** — say this explicitly. Big trust boost.

## 10. Icon checklist (don't skip — icon CTR is a top ranking signal)

- Solid dark background (#0B0E18 or pure black)
- One bold ghost silhouette OR a single glowing red eye
- Avoid: text on the icon, more than 2 colours, gradients that look muddy at 48dp
- Test at 48px — if it's unreadable, redesign. Most "ghost detector" icons
  in Play are busy and unmemorable. Win on simplicity.
